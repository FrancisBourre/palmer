Triggered when IOC process change his state.

  • ApplicationLoaderEvent.LOAD_STATE
  • ApplicationLoaderEvent.PARSE_STATE
  • ApplicationLoaderEvent.DLL_STATE
  • ApplicationLoaderEvent.RSC_STATE
